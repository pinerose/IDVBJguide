Pass card Lv.1

You can buy this card for 300 Chips.
If you want to use this card, you should touch to throw to other player in its large range.
If the player is hit by this card, you can pass the card which you selected to the player.

Pass card Lv.2

You can buy this card for 500 Chips.
If you want to use this card, you just select a player.
When you select the player, the card which you chose is passed to the player.

How to avoid the pass card?

There are two ways to avoid the pass card(avoid all range card). First, turn around the structure. You can turn around any structure.
The structure will protect you from any cards.
Finally, use the wand. his other self will protect you from the cards.

How to throw undeviatingly?

First, throw the card when you feel sure that the card will hit the target without fail.
Second, throw the card in tha flat land. any structure dosen't exist there.
Finally, never throw the card when decode the cipher machine together.
The cipher Machine can block your card.
The best way of hit with a card, throw the card to hunters.
