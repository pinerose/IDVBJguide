Reveal card(Cards)

The picture will be added in this page.

Reveal card Lv.1

You can buy this card for 10 Chips.
When you use this card, you select a player and reveal a Card in the player's Hand.
You can buy this card once a round.

Reveal card Lv.2

You can buy this card for 40 Chips.
When you use this card, you select a play and reveal every Card in the player's Hand.
You can but this card once a round.
It is sometimes used to check the other player's Hand in Deciding Round.

notice

You can select the player when the time between the purshit and the settlement.
