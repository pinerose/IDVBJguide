Shuffle card Lv.1

You can buy this card for 100 Chips.
If you want to use this card, you should touch to throw to other player in its short range.
If the player is hit by this card, your deck and the player's deck are shuffled.

Shuffle card Lv.2

You can buy this card for 200 Chips.
If you want to use this card, you should touch to throw to other player in its large range.
If the player is hit by this card, your deck and the player's deck are shuffled.

Shuffle card Lv.3

You can buy this card for 300 Chips.
If you want to use this card, you just select two players.
When you select the players, the system shuffle their deck.
You can select the players when the time between the purshit and the settlement.

Reminders

Exchange card and Shuffle card cannot guarantee the user's interests.
Think carefully before you use this card.
