What is Blackjack?


Blackjack gameplay is based on the rules of blackjack.
Each of five players will battle to control the points of their Hand.
Each round, players who exceed 21 points will be eliminated, and the last player standing wins.
On the other hand, players with exactly 21 points will win immediately.

when does Blackjack mode open?

blackjack gameplay is opened during every 10:00A.M. ~ 12:00P.M. , 14:00P.M. ~ 19:00P.M. , and 21:00P.M. ~ 24:00A.M.

What is Blackjack ranking?

Blackjack ranking is divided into "Season ranking" and "Weekly ranking".
System finds the players on the basis of season point.
And, players are able to obtain rewards after reaching a certain level on Blackjack Weekly Ranking.

About Blackjack ranking prizes

the weekly points will be reset every Sunday night at 23:59:59.
Rewards obtained will be sent to players via in-game mail.

Reaching top 1-100 in Weekly rank will reward players with:
5 rank tresures corresponding to the seasons and 5 memory sphere corresponding to the last season.

Reaching top 101-1000 in Weekly Rank will reward players with:
3 rank tresures corresponding to the seasons and 3 memory sphere corresponding to the last season.
