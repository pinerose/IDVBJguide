Regular Round

1. Deal

Players are unable to move and Blackjack will deal each player a Card.
When Blackjack deal each player a Card, the Cipher Machines will spawn in Map immediately.

2. Split

This stage lasts 5 seconds. players are able to move.
The goal is collecting chips by decoding Cipher machines.

3. Transform

The player with the highest points becomes the Hunter and will be unable to move until the transform is complete, while other players are free to move around.
this stage lasts 10 seconds.

4. Pursuit

This stage lasts for up to 2 minutes.
During this stage, the Hunter should try to knock down as many Survivors to gain more Chips(Hunters can gain 50 chips when he/she knock down a Survivors), and make a Survivor draw a Card from his Hand.
At the start and 65-seconds mark of each round, the Hunter is able to see all Survivors on the entire Map for a period of time.

5. Settlement

This is the Round Settlement stage.
Players exceeding 21 points will be eliminated and their Cards will be returned to deck.
After the Settlement, the next round will begin.

Special Round

Blackjack

When a player reaches 21 points at any time, the round will end immediately and skip the Settlement stage for a Special Round : "Blackjack".
This round will last for up to 1 minute. If the player is able to retain 21 points for the entire duration, he/she will automatically win the round;
otherwise, the Settlement stage will begin and the next round will start after that.

Deciding round

When there are only 2 players left, they will enter the "Deciding Round".
This round lasts for up to 1 minutes.
In the Deciding Round, after the Hunter knocks down the Survivor, the Hunter can choose to end the round immediately or ask the Survivor to draw a Card and then enter the Settlement stage.
Even if any player reach 21 points in this round, the round won't end.
