3. Cowboy, Kevin Ayuso

Decoding speedKiting abilityHealing speed
■ ■ ■ □ □ □ □■ ■ ■ ■ ■ □ □■ ■ ■ ■ □ □ □
He has a Lasso every round, and Lasso has 4 energies.
The energy spent when using the Lasso depends on What is Lassoed.
Lassoing Pallets and Cipher Machines reguires 2 energies, while 1 energy is required for other Lassoed items.
When you Lassoing the Pallets or Cipher Machines, you move as fast as Dash hit.
But Lasso is difficult to use, so I recommand you practice this in custom match before playing the cowboy in Blackjack.
The recovering speed of Hunter stunned by Cowboy is decreased by 20%.
And his decoding speed is decreased by 10%.
(increase the time needed for Cowboy to decode is increased by 4.4 seconds.)

"Don't Lasso to die with players."
