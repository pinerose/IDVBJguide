1. The Mind's eye, Helena Adams

Decoding speedKiting abilityvbing speed

The mind's eye has 1 Cane every round, and when she use the Strike, she can see Hunter as an aura by the settlement stage.
Mind's eye can hunter's movement so she can know the time to stealth.
And her decoding speed is increased by 30%.
(reduce the time needed for the Cipher Machine to decode by 9.2 seconds.)
But her vaulting speed is decreased by 30%.
Her performance depends on the player's stealth skill.

2. Priestess, Fiona Gilman

Decoding speedKiting abilityHealing speed
■ ■ ■ □ □ □ □■ ■ ■ ■ ■ □ □■ ■ ■ ■ ■ ■ ■
She has 2 Holy Keys when the round is started, and the Holy Key is formed once every 70 seconds.
The time needed for Priestess to heal decreased by 30%
(reduce the time needed for priestess to heal by 5.9 seconds.)
But, Her decoding speed is decreased by 10%, and Her vault speed is decreased by 10%.
(increase the time needed for Cipher Machine to decode by 4.4 seconds.)
And, the Chance of triggering and difficulty of Calibration while decoding Cipher Machine are increased by 30%.
Her item's performance depends on how it is used.

3. Prospector, Norton Campbell

Decoding speedKiting abilityTreating speed
■ ■ ■ ■ □ □ □■ ■ ■ ■ ■ □ □■ ■ ■ ■ ■ ■ □
He has 1 Magnet when the round is started, and the Magnet is formde once a minute.
His movement speed will increased by 50% for 2 seconds when a Lockers or Cipher Machines are within a certain range.
And, his healing speed is increased by 20%.
(reduce the time for Prospector to heal by 3.8 seconds.)
His chance of triggering a calibration during decoding is increased by 50%, and Calibration difficulty is increased by 30%.
So, Hunter sometime teleport the Cipher machine which you decode when you do QTE.
His item's performance depends on how it is used.

4. Perfumer, Vera Nair

Decoding speedKiting abilityTreating speed
■ ■ ■ ■ □ □ □■ ■ ■ ■ ■ ■ □■ ■ □ □ □ □ □
She has 3 Perfume every round. When you use the Perfume, your location is recoded and you teleport there if you use the Perfume again in at least 5 seconds. this time is not into the cooldown.
You can use the Perfume every 10~15 seconds.
But in Blackjack, Hunter can knock down the Survivor with normal attack.
And the time needed for Perfumer to heal increased by 30%.
(increase the time for Perfumer to heal by 6.9 seconds.)
Her item's performance depends on how it is used.

How to use Perfume?

I recommand you use the Perfume for mindgame.
For example, use the perfume when you are chased and the Window or Pallet is within a certain range. So, Hunter must choose between the two way.
If Hunter chase you still, teleport the perfume to recorded location.
Otherwise, go to next kiting zone.
